About the Event

The 2026 World Cup is an international soccer tournament that will be hosted jointly by the United States, Canada, and Mexico. It marks the first time the tournament will feature 48 teams, expanding from the traditional 32. As one of the most watched sporting events in the world, it brings together fans from every continent to celebrate the spirit of the game. The event is aimed at soccer enthusiasts of all ages, from lifelong supporters to new fans discovering the sport.
